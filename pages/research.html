Research


1. Home Page

Both of the website looks somewhat similar at first glance. In the main section, both the owner has introducted themselves with a picture of them on the right side of the website. But both of them completely differ from each other. In the website on the right side, the owner has added a short description of who he is and a small introduction of his working experience. The reference website has a yellow and dark brownish accent whereas the website on the right hand side has neon blue and dark bluish accent.

2. CV Page

The idea of designing the CV page came from the above reference website. Idea of adding the basic personal information in the first division, education history, basic and technical skill and the work experience came from this website. The website on the right side has percentage bar for the technical skill which is quite unique and attractive. It also has a internal navigation bar with buttons to navigate through the different section of the CV that provides easy and faster interface for the user.

3. Blog Page

The blog page of both of the website does not look similar. But the idea of designing this blog page was solely developed by taking ideas from the reference website. The idea of adding the heading, the author and the blog released date, an appropriate image about the blog content, and then only adding the blog content came from the reference website. Adding the recent post on the right hand side of the webpage to navigate through all the blog post was also developed from looking at the reference website. Both of the website has whitish accent with only a slight variation in the white contrast, the font, text color, and the overall view of the website.

4. Contact Page

Most of the design of the contact page of the webpage in the right were inspired from the reference website. The contact info on the right side of the reference website has all the basic contact info thats the same on the right side webpage. Both of the website has a form for the direct contact between the owner and the visitor. Though both may have similar content, they are completely different. The form design, the contect of the contact info and the overall design of the websites are different.

5. Navigation bar

The design for the navigation bar for the website was highly inspired from the above reference wbsite. Both of the website has similar navigation bar. The position of the logo and the navigation is almost same. But they differ in many aspects. The website on the right hand side goes transparent upon some scrolling which the nav bar of the reference website lacks. Each of the nav buttons are hilighted with a white shadow and has a hover effect with change in the shadow color. The website on the right has a distinct effect for the active navigation button which the reference website lacks.

Introduction to Technology

15 January Zerod

The society has been changed a lot after the evolution of technology. Before the arrival of modern technology, life was hard and doing everyday works consumed way too much of our time than it foes in the present time. The access to education, medicine, industry, transportation etc. has been simplified due to modern day technology. Due to the convenience and efficiency provided by technology, our lives have improved significantly. With so many advantages and comfort, come the disadvantages too. Technology is being used wrongly these days resulting in seroius problems in the lives of the people. One of the greatest achievement of the modern advanced technology is the Internet. The main advantage of the Internet is its ability to connect billions of computers and devices to each other.

The Internet

14 January Zerod

The Internet is a global wide area network that connects computer systems across the world. Internet has become increasing popular for many uses such as business marketing, online shopping and education and every other aspect. It is the main source of communication and connecting with each other. It is a network that consists of millions of private, public, academic, business and government networks of local to global scope that are linked by a broad area of electronic, wireless and optical networking technologies. Internet is known to be one of the most innovative outcome of the modern technology. We use internet daily in our life. Everthing that we do has internet involved in it. That is the reason why it has become a part of our life and a life without internet is impossible.

Positive impact of the Internet Technology

13 January Zerod

The internet has played a very important role especially in the communication department. Internet has made it easiers for students to research through many articles, videos and discussions published on the internet. Internet has made communication easier, cheaper, faster and reliable with the ability to connect people from opposite part of the world easily. It has made possible to earn online through writing, filling up forms, or even just clicking away on advertisements in the internet. Business transaction has been made faster with the help of internet. Buyers can surf online and order the products they want to buy with no need to go to a physical store. It has hugely saved on the travel cost as people can just surf through the internet and get to know about various places without having to go there.

Negative impact of the Internet Technology

12 January Zerod

With so many advantages and comfort, come the disadvantages too. Internet is being used imprudently these days resulting in affecting the society negatively and complicating our lives. Online games can be addictive to children, young adults and even adults that ultimately results in slacking off their works. People that are so involved in the internet have forgotten how to interact with their family, friends and coworkers. Phishing and online fraud are some of the main negative impact of the internet that has robbed many people off their house and ruined many relationships.
